What is Panchanga for? It is the Hindu almanac for any day — use it to pick good days & times: tithi decides festivals and fasting (Ekadashi, Purnima), the day's nakshatra decides muhurta suitability, and Rahu Kala / Yamaganda are the hours to avoid starting anything important. Abhijit (around noon) is the all-purpose safe window. Odisha extras: the Odia solar masa and Sankranti festivals are shown too.
